DE-RescueBeam

Directed Energy Wireless Power System

ATRC School Program – Directed Energy Challenge

Project Overview

DE-RescueBeam is a humanitarian application of directed energy technology. The system wirelessly transmits energy from a base station to rescue drones and emergency sensors in disaster zones.

The goal is to extend mission duration, reduce battery limitations, and improve rescue efficiency while maintaining strict safety controls.

Prototype Description

The Python simulation demonstrates:

  • Battery drain over time
  • Moving drones and stationary sensors
  • Smart target selection (lowest battery priority)
  • Beam range limitation
  • Human safety exclusion zone
